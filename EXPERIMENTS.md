**Finding (‚úÖ confound resolved):** synth-only fine-tuning improved *everything*, including **non-paintings** (+2.6 full GAP) ‚ÄĒ surprising; the prediction was forgetting. Because non-paintings also rose, it's not pure forgetting ‚ÄĒ the diverse renders taught **transferable lighting/viewpoint/glass invariance** that helps real photos broadly. So the lift is **real and attributable to the synthetic data itself**, not to extra training, and it clears the paper's best single model (36.1). This is the headline result for contribution 1.

### EXP-5 ‚ÄĒ step 5, new method ūüü°
**Additional experiment ‚ÄĒ cross-domain pair mining.** New `--pairs_type cross_domain_pos+new_neg`
(`code/utils/datasets.py`): each anchor's positive is the *closest same-class sample in the OTHER
domain* (studio‚ÜĒsynthetic) via `mine_positive` on the cross-domain subset; classes with no
cross-domain partner fall back to standard `new_pos`, singletons self-pair. Directly optimizes the
studio‚Üígallery-photo bridge. Fires for the **4,952** painting classes that have both a studio and a
synthetic image (verified on `data/gt_aug`). Mining the *closest* render also sidesteps the broken
grazing-view renders (they sit far from the studio image). Recipe = `slurm/train_synth.slurm` (from-SWSL,
10 ep, combined manifest) with only the pairs_type changed ‚Üí **clean A/B vs scratch+synth** (job
7330059). Job `slurm/train_crossdomain.slurm` ‚Üí **7332307 (queued**, waiting on a GPU). Mining logic
smoke-tested (tiny mixed manifest, asserts cross/fallback/singleton/negatives). Eval TBD (epoch-10
ckpt ‚Üí `data/models/r18SWSL_crossdomain/`; needs the same eval tweak as scratch+synth, not the
`slurm/extract_eval_ft.slurm` epoch-5 globber).

**Main method:** see EXP-6 (pivoted to DINOv3 + geometric re-rank).

### EXP-6 ‚ÄĒ DINOv3 backbone + geometric re-rank (the new-method thread) ūüü°
Pivoted step 5 to a foundation backbone after finding DINOv3 already ~doubles the R18 GAP. All
eval'd in OUR pipeline (canonical kNN-softmax GAP). DINOv3 features **reused** from the sibling
`art-research` repo (Met split verified **byte-identical**, md5). **DINOv3 ZS on Met is the DINOv3
paper's own claim** (Met GAP = DINOv2 40.0 **+10.8** ‚Čą 50.8) ‚ÄĒ NOT our contribution; our delta is the
geometric re-rank on top.

**Step 2 ‚ÄĒ DINOv3 reproduced in our pipeline** (`scripts/build_dinov3_pkl.py` + `eval_fullgrid.py`,
`slurm/eval_dinov3.slurm` job 7332349): assemble raw DINOv3 CLS feats (aspect512, 4096-d) ‚Üí our PCAw
4096‚Üí512 + faiss kNN + GAP. Reproduces the paper ‚Üí bridge faithful; earlier k=1 "0.68" was the
degeneracy artifact (our full grid picks K=5).

| backbone (frozen, zero-shot) | GAP | GAP‚ĀĽ | ACC |
|---|--:|--:|--:|
| R18-SWSL (our step-1) | 35.97 | 52.14 | 54.64 |
| DINOv3-ViT-L | 48.16 | 72.14 | 77.07 |
| DINOv3-7B (best K=5, ŌĄ=20) | 52.11 | 75.46 | 81.95 |

**Step 3 ‚ÄĒ geometric re-rank for GAP** (C2 PatchMatch = mutual-NN + RANSAC on DINOv3 ViT-L patches,
over the strong CLS top-50; `scripts/patchmatch_rerank.py` job 7332350 ‚Üí PM scores saved to
`data/rerank/pm_scores_*.npz`). Goal: reject distractors (DINOv3's weak spot ‚ÄĒ GAP‚ĀĽ 72 vs GAP 48 =
24-pt gap). PM separates true (maxPM mean **34**) from distractors (**21**).
- *Take 1 ‚ÄĒ additive into pre-softmax sim:* **NULL** (tuner picks őĽ=0). The softmax saturates (conf‚Čą1
  for true AND distractor), washing the signal out.
- *Take 2 ‚ÄĒ fuse into the CONFIDENCE, ACC frozen* (`scripts/rerank_confidence_fusion.py`,
  `slurm/rerank_fusion.slurm`): **works.**

| DINOv3-ViT-L (Met protocol) | GAP | GAP‚ĀĽ | ACC |
|---|--:|--:|--:|
| baseline (CLS kNN) | 48.16 | 72.14 | 77.07 |
| **+ geom gate** `conf¬∑(1+w¬∑maxPM/g0)`, w=0.25 | **53.07** | **74.69** | 77.07 |
| + geom RRF, w=0.25 | 56.15 | 66.40 | 77.07 |

**Gate improves BOTH GAP (+4.9) and GAP‚ĀĽ (+2.55), ACC unchanged** ‚ÄĒ clean distractor rejection, no
tradeoff, and ViT-L+gate (53.07) already edges DINOv3-7B ZS (52.11). RRF's +8 GAP is a val-overfit
mirage (K=1, GAP‚ĀĽ ‚ąí5.7). **Next:** apply gate to the **7B** stage-1 (baseline 52.11) for the headline

art-research DINOv3 feats+patches; planar-homography geometry is iffy for 3D (non-painting) exhibits.

### EXP-7 ‚ÄĒ DINOv3 embedding structure of the synthetic dataset ‚úÖ
What organizes the **frozen DINOv3 ViT-L** (aspect512 CLS, L2-norm/cosine) embedding space of the
24,760 synthetic renders ‚ÄĒ camera angle vs procedural hyperparameters ‚ÄĒ and how does it sit relative
to the real domains? Real side **reuses art-research's identical ViT-L Met features** (same model +
aspect512 preprocessing): studio sources `MET/<id>/0.jpg` (4,952, paired) + real painting test queries
(**148**, committed `Classification=="Paintings"` ‚ÄĒ the project-wide def). Pipeline: `scripts/extract_synth_dino.py` (GPU job **7333958**, 24,760√ó1024
in **2 min @275 img/s**, batched since renders are all 512¬≤) ‚Üí `scripts/assemble_real_dino.py` +
`scripts/analyze_synth_dino.py` (CPU `slurm/analysis_synth_dino.slurm`, job 7333977). Factors parsed by
`scripts/synth_meta.py`. Artifacts: `data/synth_dino/analysis/{summary.json, 6 PNGs}`.
**Standalone write-up with all figures: [`experiments/synth-embedding-analysis/`](experiments/synth-embedding-analysis/README.md).**

**1) Content dominates; angle is the strong secondary axis; procedural nuisances are weakly encoded.**

| factor (synthetic only) | lin-probe | kNN | silhouette | chance |
|---|--:|--:|--:|--:|
| camera angle (5) | **0.990** | 0.72 | 0.02 | 0.20 |
| floor material (5) | 0.59 | 0.50 | ~0 | 0.21 |
| placard-x quartile | 0.55 | 0.54 | ~0 | 0.25 |
| canvas aspect quartile *(content-correlated)* | 0.73 | 0.67 | ~0 | 0.25 |

kNN(k=10) neighbour composition ‚ÄĒ **de-confounded** (floor is randomized **once per painting**, so all
5 views share it ‚Üí same-painting‚áísame-floor; each painting has 1 view/angle ‚Üí same-painting‚áídiff-angle):
**same-painting 0.247** (chance 0.0002, ~1500√ó); among *different*-painting neighbours **same-angle
0.68** (3.4√ó chance) vs **same-floor 0.28** (1.4√ó). Read: DINOv3 keys on **painting identity** (tight
per-artwork clusters in t-SNE; angle near-perfectly *linearly* separable but **not** isolated blobs,
silhouette‚Čą0); **angle** is the dominant cross-painting organizer; **floor/placard barely encoded**.

**2) Clean, large domain gap ‚ÄĒ and the renders are "too clean".** Linear separability: studio/synth/query
3-way **0.995**; studio‚ÜĒsynth 0.992, studio‚ÜĒquery 0.973, synth‚ÜĒquery 0.973 ‚ÄĒ synthetic is its **own**
region. Centroid cosine dist: **studio‚ÜĒquery 0.22** (the real gap); synth **front is closest to studio
(0.15)** ‚ÄĒ *closer than the real queries are* ‚ÄĒ and **no** synthetic view lands closer to
the real-query centroid than studio already is (front‚ÜĒquery 0.23 ‚Čą studio‚ÜĒquery 0.22). So in frozen-
DINOv3 space the renders add **viewpoint/glass/lighting variation** but do **not** reproduce the real
phone-photo shift ‚Üí EXP-4's synthetic gain is more plausibly **augmentation/invariance** than domain-matching.

**3) Framing bug corroborated independently** (DINOv3 + cosine vs EXP-3's R18 retrieval): `right upper`
renders sit far from their **own** studio source (cos **0.44** vs front **0.84**) and far in centroid
space (**0.62** from studio); per-view cos-to-source bottoms out exactly where EXP-3 R@1 does.

**Caveats:** frozen DINOv3 ViT-L (not the FT-R18 of EXP-1/4, nor 7B); L2-normed CLS cosine (not the
eval's train-fit PCAw); light randomization not recoverable from `metadata.json`; mid-view cos-to-source
doesn't perfectly track R@1 (retrievability = *discriminability*, not raw similarity; EXP-3 used R18).

## Gotchas & decisions
- **Eval must tune the full K grid** (`scripts/eval_fullgrid.py`), not the README's `--autotune` (K=1, ‚Üí GAP ‚Čą 23).
- `--net r18_sw-sup` for training, `r18_contr_loss_gem_fc_swsl` (+`--netpath`) for extraction.
- faiss on **CPU** (H100 sm_90 gap); identical exact-IP results.
- **Checkpoint load needs `weights_only=False`** (torch 2.8).
- **Fine-tune LR:** load weights via `--init_weights` @ 1e-7; literal `--resume` = decayed 1e-8 = no-op.
- **Eval DB = original 397k studio images** for every run (so all models are comparable; synthetic is added to *training* only, never the DB).
- **Synthetic camera-rig bug:** `right upper` grazing view (R@1 1.4%) ‚ÄĒ fix + regenerate before per-angle synthetic claims.
- GPU = `--gres=gpu:h100:1` on `tesla`. Authors' artifacts under `data/authors/` (separate from our `data/models/`).
